Live Drivers Opt-In

This feature allows you to view the live drivers of prospective partners on their profile page.
Only companies who opt-in to this feature will be able to view live drivers and will also have their own drivers shared with other prospective partners.
This will help partners make a more informed choice when choosing to partner with each other.

Please Note: Enabling this feature will allow other partners who have also enabled the live drivers feature to view your live drivers.

Activation/Deactivation will take 12 hours.
